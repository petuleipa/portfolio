PROJECTS

Store pages can be found through the links in the titles.


JoyDepress (2025)

JoyDepress is a point & click trash sorting simulation game made with Unreal Engine. You must sort trash from a closed down theme park and make enough profit to expand your production and keep up with the expenses, or face the consequences of failure.

This was my first full project with Unreal Engine and I worked as a gameplay programmer focused mainly on the machinery and game logic. Code was a mix of C++ and Unreal Blueprints. Published in itch.io.

Night of the Living Skurre (2025)

A 3D third person action-platformer made with Unity, where you play as a skurre on a quest to save their forest from a lumberjack company. Your goal is to drive the lumberjack insane by setting elaborate traps in their cabin or alternatively just pelting them with pinecones.

I worked on the logic for multiple traps and player-environment interactions, as well as setting up the environment in engine. Published on Steam and itch.io.

Corpse Crafter (2024)

A 2D mouse-controlled tower graveyard defence game. You must defend yourself against angry villagers by digging graves and creating zombies to fight for you. Made with Unity.

Gameplay programming of various features in a small team of four people. First time rigging and making character animations using Unitys animation system. Published on itch.io.

The Everhart Manor (2024)

A Top-down 2D mobile game for android, where you explore an old mansion and catch ghosts. Story-driven with puzzles and environment interactions. Made with Unity.

I was the lead programmer and in charge of many of the interactable puzzles and game progression logic.
